    A little about us

    KL Executive (KLX) brings you a refreshing and inspiring experience in Makati. Located on Gamboa Street, fronting the Legazpi Active Park and Washington Sycip Park, our serviced residences offer a blend of the serenity of nature, proximity to shopping, and the convenience of being in the heart of business.
